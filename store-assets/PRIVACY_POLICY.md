# Privacy Policy — TikTok Skipper

**Last updated:** 2026-04-03

This Privacy Policy describes how the **TikTok Skipper** Chrome extension (“the Extension”) handles information.

## Data collection

The Extension **does not collect**, **does not store**, and **does not transmit** personal data to the developer’s servers.

## Local processing

When you visit `https://www.tiktok.com/`, the Extension runs locally in your browser to:

- Detect the active `<video>` element in the page
- Adjust the video’s playback time (`currentTime`) when you click the on-screen buttons

This processing happens **entirely on your device**.

## Third parties

The Extension does not embed third-party analytics or advertising libraries.

TikTok’s own services are governed by TikTok’s terms and privacy policy.

## Changes

This policy may be updated when the Extension’s behavior changes. The “Last updated” date will be revised accordingly.

## Contact

Please use the contact information provided on the Extension’s Chrome Web Store listing or the linked GitHub repository.
